Conflict
Resolution
Phrases

Addressing workplace conflicts impacts productivity and well-being. Effective resolution involves clear communication, active listening, and cooperative solutions. Our infographic offers key words and techniques for conflict resolution.

"Let's deal with
this together"

Promote cooperative conflict resolution, fostering unity and shared accountability among team members.

“I Understand
how you feel”

Show empathy and gratitude for the other person's perspective. Acknowledging their emotions increases trust and encourages open, productive communication.


”I appreciate
your input”

Acknowledge and value others' efforts, even in disagreement. Promote positivity, respect, and open communication with this approach.

“Let's step back and
consider the
big picture”

Gain clarity and overcome biases by adopting a broader perspective. Shift focus from individual to group goals by zooming out.

"I'm sorry if my actions
contributed to
the conflict"

Acknowledge your role in the conflict and exhibit humility. Encourage a culture of accountability and growth through this approach.



Encourage effective workplace dispute resolution through collaboration, polite communication, and active listening. Utilize these phrases to create a positive, productive atmosphere, strengthening teamwork and achieving better outcomes.
