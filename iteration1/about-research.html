Our Research

Intergenerational Communication of Clerical Workforce in Metro Manila

In the world of clerical work, communication is vital, which comes in various forms and mediums including social media, digital media and other innovative and fast paced ways to transact with information faster. With these, companies hire newer generations which blend with the current and older cohorts all interacting in a workspace. With this, this study aims to describe the current status of intergenerational communication in Metro Manila. This involves describing the saturation of generational distribution in different sectors and the problem that may arise with the status quo.

In this research, the proponents have discovered that Millenials are the most prominent in the workplace considering their skills currently fitting to the latest communication necessities. Parallel to this, most preferred using Facebook or Messenger, and Texting as their way of connecting to colleagues. Challenges such as stereotypes are not observable to many but in few cases, such barriers hinder generations from communicating. Oral communication, and face to face discussions are preferred by the population as well which creates a diversity in discourse preferences.

To promote intergenerational communication, the proponents may take advantage of the motivators identified by the target population and implement their project: BridGen. The researchers took the generational gap, communication preferences, behaviors, challenges and motivators to promote and give awareness to the current status of clerical intergenerational communication. Through "BridGen" is an innovative Online graphic hyper narrative that acts as an educational tool that aims to simulate the experience of the workplace through an engaging and immersive storytelling format. It combines visual novel elements, interactive decision-making, and graphic design to create an insightful and entertaining experience for users. BridGen is accompanied by an informative website where users can download the visual novel and learn more about the project.
