INTERACTIVE VISUAL NOVEL

"Harmonyville" is an interactive visual novel set in Harmonyville, where players assume the role of Alex, navigating communication barriers in InnovateTech's diverse workplace. Through decision-making and problem-solving, players unravel generational dynamics, foster collaboration, and learn to harmonize diverse perspectives for meaningful connections in the corporate world.
