INTERACTIVE VISUAL NOVEL

Welcome to "Harmonyville: Cross-Generational Connections"! In this hyper narrative story, you will step into the shoes of Alex, a new employee at Clearview Office Solutions, and navigate through the struggles a newly hired employee may encounter in the workplace.

Instructions

  1. Getting Started:

    2. Choose the responses and actions for Alex at different decision points in the story. Your choices will influence the narrative and determine the outcome of Alex's journey.

  2. Making Choices:

    3. Throughout the game, you will encounter multiple choices that Alex must make. Each choice will have consequences, leading to different paths and outcomes.

  3. Interacting with Characters:

    4. As you begin, you will interact with different characters, such as Emma, Sarah, Mark, Emily, and Rachel.

  4. Advancing the Story:
    • Your choices will determine the flow of the story and lead to different timelines and outcomes for Alex's journey.
    • Keep in mind that the way you interact with colleagues and address communication challenges will impact Alex's growth and the overall workplace atmosphere.
  5. Multiple Endings:
    • The hypernarrative features multiple endings based on the choices you make. Your decisions will determine whether Alex's journey results in a good ending, neutral and bad endings.
    • There is no right or wrong choice; every decision you make will shape Alex's experiences and the workplace culture.
  6. Embrace Learning:

    7. Embrace the learning experience, and consider how the insights gained from Alex's journey can be applied to real-life situations.

  7. You can replay the Hypernarrative!
    • Feel free to replay the hypernarrative and make different choices to explore various outcomes and storylines.
    • Discover the diverse possibilities and strive to create a positive and inclusive workplace environment for Alex and their colleagues.
